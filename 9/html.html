Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. The company designs, manufactures, and markets consumer electronics, software, and services. It is known for its flagship products like the iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, and more. Apple is one of the world's largest technology companies and has a significant global presence.
